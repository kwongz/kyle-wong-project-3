Water is arguably the most important ingredient for life. Yet we tend to overlook our daily water consumption and as a result face adverse health effects.

  • 1% dehydration can cause significant decrease in metabolism, resulting in difficulty in losing weight
  • Differentiating between hunger and thirst is difficult, drinking water may resolve your urge for snacking
  • One of the easiest preventions for kidney stones is drinking enough water
